<p><strong>The Agricultural Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Agriculture Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial agricultural tires are constructed with cords at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing greater flexibility and a larger footprint for improved traction and less soil compaction. Bias (Crossply) agricultural tires, on the other hand, have cords that run diagonally across the tire, offering a more rigid structure suitable for heavy loads and rough terrain. Both types cater to different needs within the agricultural sector, with radial tires excelling in modern farming practices and bias tires being a cost-effective option for certain applications.</p></p>
