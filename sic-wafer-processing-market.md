<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The SiC Wafer Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slicing</li><li>Edge Grinding</li><li>Lapping</li><li>Polishing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC wafer processing market can be categorized into four types: slicing, edge grinding, lapping, and polishing. Slicing involves cutting SiC wafers from a larger ingot using a diamond wire saw. Edge grinding is the process of refining the sidewalls of the sliced SiC wafers. Lapping further improves flatness and surface quality by using abrasives on a rotating workpiece. Lastly, polishing enhances the smoothness and cleanness of the SiC wafers by removing any remaining imperfections. These processing methods are crucial for ensuring high-quality SiC wafers used in various applications.</p></p>
