<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Touch Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Film Touch Panels</li><li>Capacitive Touch Panels</li><li>Projected Capacitive Touch Panels</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Touch Panels</li><li>Optical Touch Panels (Infrared Optical Imaging Touch Panels)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen market is segmented into different types based on the technology used for touch functionality. Resistive Film Touch Panels use two conductive layers to detect touch. Capacitive Touch Panels use the body's electrical charge to register touch. Projected Capacitive Touch Panels have a glass surface that can detect multiple touch points. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Touch Panels use ultrasonic waves to detect touch. Optical Touch Panels use sensors to detect interruption in infrared light beams for touch registration.</p></p>
