<p><p>Civil aircraft ambulifts, also known as air ambulifts or aircraft boarding systems, are specially designed airport ground support vehicles used to facilitate the transfer of passengers with reduced mobility (PRM) from the airport terminal to the aircraft and vice versa. These ambulifts are equipped with advanced features like hydraulic lifts, adjustable cabins, wheelchair restraint systems, and other support mechanisms to ensure safe and comfortable transportation of individuals with mobility limitations.</p><p>The civil aircraft ambulifts market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. The increasing number of air travelers with reduced mobility, the rising demand for seamless airport operations, and the growing emphasis on ensuring inclusive travel experiences for passengers are the key factors driving the market growth. Moreover, technological advancements in ambulift designs and innovations in passenger handling systems have further contributed to the market expansion.</p><p>Several key trends have been observed in the civil aircraft ambulifts market. The integration of automated and electronic systems in ambulifts has improved operational efficiency and safety standards.
