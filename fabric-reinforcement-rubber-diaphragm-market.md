<p><p>Fabric reinforcement rubber diaphragms are used in various industries for applications requiring high strength and durability. The two main types of fabric reinforcement are polyamide and polyester. Polyamide reinforcement diaphragms are known for their excellent dimensional stability and resistance to high temperatures, making them ideal for demanding industrial environments. On the other hand, polyester reinforcement diaphragms offer greater flexibility and fatigue resistance, making them suitable for applications where repeated flexing is required. Both types play a crucial role in providing reliable performance and longevity in diaphragm applications.</p></p>
