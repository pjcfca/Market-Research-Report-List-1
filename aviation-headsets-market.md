<p><strong>The Aviation Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNR headsets</li><li>ANR headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation headsets market is segmented into two main types: Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) headsets and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) headsets. PNR headsets use cushioning materials to block out external noise, providing basic noise reduction for pilots. On the other hand, ANR headsets use advanced technology to actively cancel out noise, offering superior noise reduction and clearer communication in loud environments. Both types cater to the specific needs and preferences of pilots in the aviation industry.</p></p>
