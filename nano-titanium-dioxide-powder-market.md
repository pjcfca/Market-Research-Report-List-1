<p><p>Nano Titanium Dioxide Powder Market can be classified into two main types: Rutile Type and Anatase Type. Rutile Type nano titanium dioxide powder contains a higher proportion of rutile crystals, which provide superior UV absorption and photocatalytic properties. On the other hand, Anatase Type nano titanium dioxide powder is characterized by a higher surface area and improved photocatalytic activity. Both types are widely used in various industries, such as cosmetics, paints, and coatings, for their excellent UV protection and photocatalytic properties.</p></p>
