<p><p>The market for general type dehumidifiers is divided into two types: mechanical type and electronic type. Mechanical type dehumidifiers operate by extracting moisture from the air using a fan and refrigeration system. They are generally large and bulky, suitable for commercial use. On the other hand, electronic type dehumidifiers utilize the Peltier effect to remove moisture using a small electric fan. They are compact and commonly used for household purposes. Both types aim to reduce humidity levels to create a more comfortable and healthier indoor environment.</p></p>
