<p><p>Automotive Brake Actuation Systems include various types such as Brake Booster, Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs), and Brake Fluid Reservoir. </p><p>Brake Boosters enhance braking performance by increasing the force applied to the master cylinder. Tandem Master Cylinders (TMCs) have two separate hydraulic circuits for redundancy and safety. Brake Fluid Reservoirs store and supply brake fluid to the hydraulic system to maintain proper brake function. These systems work together to ensure efficient and reliable braking in vehicles.</p></p>
