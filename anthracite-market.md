<p><p>Anthracite market includes two main types - Lump Anthracite and Anthracite Fines. Lump Anthracite is a large, pure form of anthracite coal that is used for heating and as a source of carbon in various industries. Anthracite Fines, on the other hand, are smaller, dust-like particles of anthracite coal that are commonly used in water filtration systems and as a fuel source in power plants. Both types of anthracite play a crucial role in providing energy and raw materials for different applications.</p></p>
