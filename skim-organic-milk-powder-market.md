<p><p>Skim organic milk powder is available in two market types: regular and instant. Regular skim organic milk powder is a powdered form of milk that is low in fat and produced from organic dairy sources. It requires mixing with water before consumption. On the other hand, instant skim organic milk powder is also low in fat, but has undergone a specific process to make it dissolve quickly in water without the need for additional mixing. Both types cater to consumers seeking nutritious and convenient dairy alternatives.</p></p>
