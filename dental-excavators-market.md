<p><p>Dental excavators are essential tools in dentistry used for the removal of unhealthy tooth tissues. There are two main types of dental excavators: round excavators and spoon excavators. Round excavators have a circular cutting edge that allows for precise removal of decayed or damaged parts of the tooth. On the other hand, spoon excavators have a rounded scoop-shaped tip that is primarily used for smoothing out cavity walls and removing debris. Both types of excavators play a crucial role in maintaining oral health and preparing the tooth for subsequent dental procedures.</p></p>
