<p><p>The Blood Glucose Monitoring (BGM) market comprises different types of devices used to monitor blood glucose levels. Lancets are small needles used to prick the skin for blood sampling, while Blood Glucose Testing Strips are thin pieces of material that enable blood to be applied for glucose measurement. These devices are essential for individuals with diabetes to manage their condition effectively.</p></p>
