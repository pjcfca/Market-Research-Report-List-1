<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hygrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Hygrometer</li><li>Resistive Hygrometer</li><li>Thermal Hygrometer</li><li>Gravimetric Hygrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygrometer market types include capacitive, resistive, thermal, and gravimetric hygrometers. Capacitive hygrometers measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical capacitance, while resistive hygrometers use a sensing element that changes resistance with humidity levels. Thermal hygrometers measure humidity based on the amount of heat required to evaporate moisture from an air sample. Gravimetric hygrometers measure humidity by weighing a sample before and after moisture absorption. Each type offers different advantages and is used in various industries based on specific needs.</p></p>
