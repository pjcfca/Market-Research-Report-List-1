<p><p>Image guided surgery instruments are specialized devices used during surgical procedures to provide real-time imaging and guidance to surgeons. These instruments combine surgical tools with advanced imaging technology, such as MRI, CT scan, or ultrasound, to assist surgeons in accurately navigating and positioning surgical instruments within the patient's body.</p><p>The market for image guided surgery instruments is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cancer and cardiovascular disorders, is driving the demand for minimally invasive surgeries, which in turn is fueling the adoption of image guided surgery instruments. These instruments enhance surgical precision, reduce invasiveness, minimize complications, and improve patient outcomes, leading to their increasing popularity among surgeons.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in imaging technologies and surgical instruments are further propelling market growth. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms with image guided surgery instruments has revolutionized the surgical process, enabling surgeons to make real-time decisions and perform complex surgeries with greater accuracy and precision. Furthermore, the rising geriatric population and increasing healthcare expenditure in emerging economies are expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p><p>In terms of trends, there has been a shift towards hybrid operating rooms, which integrate advanced imaging modalities and surgical instruments in a single space.
