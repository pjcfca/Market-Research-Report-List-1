<p><p>Pipe coating market offers several types of coatings for different applications. Thermoplastic coatings are applied by heating and melting the coating material onto the pipe surface. Fusion bonded epoxy coatings provide protection against corrosion. Metal coatings are used for high temperature and harsh environments. Concrete weight coatings add weight and stability to underwater pipelines. Polyurea coatings are known for their high chemical and abrasion resistance. Other types of coatings include anti-corrosion, insulating, and fire protection coatings. each type serves a specific purpose in pipe protection and longevity.</p></p>
