<p><strong>The Solid Pneumatic Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Stage</li><li>2-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid pneumatic tires market can be classified into 3-stage and 2-stage categories. The 3-stage market refers to tires with three distinct layers - an outer rubber tread, a cushion layer, and a strong inner hub. These tires are more durable and suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, the 2-stage market consists of tires with only two layers - the rubber tread and inner hub. These tires are more commonly used in light to medium-duty applications where durability is not the primary concern.</p></p>
