<p><p>Corrugated roofing sheets are available in various types, including bite type, lap joint type, and others. Bite type sheets have overlapping edges that interlock securely, providing a strong and durable roofing solution. Lap joint type sheets feature a unique design where one edge is raised while the other is recessed, allowing for easy installation and enhanced water tightness. Other types include self-sealing sheets, trapezoidal sheets, and standing seam sheets, offering a wide range of options for different roofing needs.</p></p>
