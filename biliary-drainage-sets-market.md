<p><p>Biliary drainage sets are medical devices used to help drain bile from the liver or gallbladder when there is a blockage. The two main types of biliary drainage sets are Biliary Drainage Catheters and Biliary Drainage Bags. Biliary Drainage Catheters are flexible tubes inserted into the bile duct to facilitate drainage, while Biliary Drainage Bags are attached to collect the drained bile. Both types of devices are essential in managing conditions such as bile duct stones, strictures, and tumors that can obstruct the flow of bile.</p></p>
