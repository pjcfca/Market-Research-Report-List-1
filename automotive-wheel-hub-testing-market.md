<p><p>Automotive wheel hub testing market encompasses various types of tests conducted for wheel hubs in the automotive industry. Performance testing involves assessing key parameters such as load capacity, durability, and impact resistance of wheel hubs. PCD testing focuses on verifying the accuracy and compatibility of the wheel hub's pitch circle diameter with the vehicle's specifications. Inertial resistance testing measures the ability of the wheel hub to withstand rotational forces. The "Others" category includes additional tests such as salt spray testing, vibration testing, and temperature testing, which further evaluate the performance and reliability of automotive wheel hubs.</p></p>
