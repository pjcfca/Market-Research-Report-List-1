<p><p>Aircraft fire extinguishers are classified into two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed fire extinguishers are permanently installed in specific areas of the aircraft and are designed to automatically release fire suppressants in the event of a fire. Portable fire extinguishers, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily carried to different areas of the aircraft for immediate use by passengers or crew members. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of passengers and crew in case of fire emergencies.</p></p>
