<p><p>The Automotive Mass Air Flow (MAF) Sensors market includes two main types: hot wire and hot film sensors. Hot wire sensors operate by measuring the amount of current needed to keep a wire at a constant temperature while hot film sensors measure air flow based on the cooling effect of air passing over a heated film. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the right amount of fuel is delivered to the engine for optimal performance and fuel efficiency in vehicles.</p></p>
