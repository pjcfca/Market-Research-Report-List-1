<p><p>Heavy Duty Diesel (HDD) Catalysts market types include Hydrocarbons (HC), Carbon Monoxide (CO), Oxides of Nitrogen (NOx), and Particulate Matter (PM). HC catalysts are designed to reduce unburned hydrocarbon emissions, while CO catalysts reduce carbon monoxide emissions. NOx catalysts aim to lower the levels of nitrogen oxides in exhaust gases, and PM catalysts target particulate matter emissions. These catalysts play a crucial role in reducing harmful pollutants from heavy-duty diesel engines, making them more environmentally friendly.</p></p>
