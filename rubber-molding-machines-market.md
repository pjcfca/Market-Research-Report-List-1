<p><p>Rubber molding machines are classified into different types based on their operational mechanism. Injection molding machines are commonly used for producing rubber parts by injecting molten rubber into a mold. Compression molding machines compress the rubber material into a desired shape using heat and pressure. Other types of rubber molding machines include transfer molding machines, liquid silicone rubber (LSR) molding machines, and vacuum molding machines. Each type of machine offers distinct benefits and is suitable for different applications in the rubber molding industry.</p></p>
