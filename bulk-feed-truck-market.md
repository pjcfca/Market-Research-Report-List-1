<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electrodynamic</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk feed trucks can be classified into three main types based on how they unload the feed: Hydraulic, Electrodynamic, and Pneumatic. Hydraulic bulk feed trucks use hydraulic power to lift and tip the truck bed, allowing the feed to flow out. Electrodynamic bulk feed trucks use an electric motor to power a conveyor belt or auger system that moves the feed out of the truck. Pneumatic bulk feed trucks use compressed air to blow the feed out of the truck through a tube or hose.</p></p>
