<p><p>Drivetrain market types refer to the different systems that deliver power from the engine to the wheels of a vehicle. Front-wheel drive (FWD) vehicles have the engine power sent to the front wheels, allowing for better traction in slippery conditions. Rear-wheel drive (RWD) vehicles have power sent to the rear wheels, providing better handling and balance. All-wheel drive (AWD) vehicles distribute power to all four wheels, offering improved traction in various driving conditions. Each drivetrain type caters to different preferences and driving needs.</p></p>
