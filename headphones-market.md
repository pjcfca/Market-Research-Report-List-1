<p><p>Headphones come in three main types - In-Ear, Over-Ear, and On-Ear. In-Ear headphones are small and lightweight, fitting snugly inside the ear canal. Over-Ear headphones have large ear cups that completely cover the ears, providing better noise isolation and sound quality. On-Ear headphones rest on the outer ears, offering a balance between portability and comfort. Each type caters to different preferences, with In-Ear being more portable, Over-Ear providing better sound quality, and On-Ear offering a compromise between the two.</p></p>
