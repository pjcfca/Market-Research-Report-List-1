<p><strong>The Knock Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Acoustic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric knock sensors detect engine knocks by converting mechanical vibrations into electrical signals. They are widely used in automotive engines for monitoring and controlling combustion processes. Acoustic knock sensors, on the other hand, detect knocks by analyzing sound waves generated during combustion. These sensors are also commonly used in automotive applications to prevent engine damage. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring optimal engine performance and efficiency by detecting and responding to knock events promptly.</p></p>
