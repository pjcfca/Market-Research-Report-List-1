<p><p>Start-Stop Technology refers to a system in vehicles that automatically shuts off the engine when the vehicle comes to a stop and restarts it when the accelerator is pressed. The market types for Start-Stop Technology include Enhanced starter, which provides extra power to restart the engine quickly; Belt-driven alternator starter (BAS), which uses a belt to restart the engine; Direct starter, which directly starts the engine without a belt; and Integrated starter generator (ISG), which combines the functions of a starter and generator for smoother operation.</p></p>
