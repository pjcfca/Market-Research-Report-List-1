<p><p>Printing Base Films Market consists of two main types: Biaxially Stretched Film and Unidirectional Stretched Film. Biaxially Stretched Film is versatile and commonly used in flexible packaging, labels, and graphic arts due to its superior strength and exceptional printability. On the other hand, Unidirectional Stretched Film is specifically designed for applications requiring high tensile strength in one direction, such as construction materials and industrial packaging.
