<p><p>Bike computers come in three main types: wired, wireless, and wireless & GPS. Wired computers are connected to the bike's sensors with a cord, providing accurate data but limited flexibility. Wireless computers use a signal to transmit data between sensors and the computer, offering more freedom of movement on the bike. Wireless & GPS computers combine the benefits of wireless technology with GPS capabilities for accurate location tracking and route planning. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of cyclists.</p></p>
