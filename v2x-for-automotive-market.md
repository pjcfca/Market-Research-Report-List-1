<p><p>V2X, or Vehicle-to-Everything communication, refers to technologies that allow vehicles to communicate with other vehicles (V2V), infrastructure (V2I), pedestrians (V2P), and other entities on the road. V2V enables cars to exchange information like speed and position to prevent accidents. V2I allows vehicles to connect with traffic signals and road signs for improved traffic flow. V2P enables vehicles to communicate with pedestrians through smartphones or wearable devices. Other markets for V2X include vehicle-to-grid (V2G) for energy management and vehicle-to-home (V2H) for smart home integration.</p></p>
