<p><strong>The Baby Wipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunlace</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Coform</li><li>Needle Punch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baby wipes market is segmented into four main types: spunlace, airlaid, coform, and needle punch. Spunlace baby wipes are made using high-pressure water jets to entangle fibers, resulting in a soft and strong wipe. Airlaid wipes are made using a process that combines air and wood pulp fibers to create a thick and absorbent wipe. Coform wipes combine wood pulp fibers with synthetic materials for a soft and durable wipe. Needle punch wipes use a process of mechanically bonding fibers for a strong and textured wipe.</p></p>
