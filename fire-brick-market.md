<p><ul><li>Unshaped (monolithic refractories)</li><li>Shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire brick market can be divided into two types: unshaped (monolithic refractories) and shaped. Unshaped fire bricks are made of a single, uniform material and are typically used for lining large industrial furnaces and boilers. Shaped fire bricks, on the other hand, are manufactured into specific shapes and sizes to fit different applications, such as lining chimneys, fireplaces, and pizza ovens. Both types of fire bricks play essential roles in providing heat resistance and insulation in various industrial and residential settings.</p></p>
