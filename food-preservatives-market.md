<p><ul><li>Antimicrobials</li><li>Antioxidants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food preservatives market is segmented into two main types: antimicrobials and antioxidants. Antimicrobials are substances that prevent the growth of bacteria, yeast, and molds in food, leading to increased shelf life. They are commonly used in products like processed meats, dairy, and beverages. On the other hand, antioxidants prevent oxidation in food, which can lead to spoilage and rancidity. They are commonly used in oils, snacks, and baked goods to extend their freshness and flavor. Both types of preservatives play a crucial role in maintaining food quality and safety.</p></p>
