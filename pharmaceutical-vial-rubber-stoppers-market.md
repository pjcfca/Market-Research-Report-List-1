<p><ul><li>Conventional Stoppers</li><li>Film-coated Stoppers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical vial rubber stoppers market comprises two main types: conventional stoppers and film-coated stoppers. Conventional stoppers are the traditional type that is commonly used in pharmaceutical packaging. They are made from a solid rubber material and provide a tight and secure seal for the vials. On the other hand, film-coated stoppers have an additional thin film layer, typically made of silicone, which enhances the compatibility between the rubber stopper and the drug. This film coating helps to minimize drug interaction with the stopper and ensures the drug's stability during storage.</p></p>
