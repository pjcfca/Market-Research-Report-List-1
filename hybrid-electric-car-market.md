<p><strong>The Hybrid Electric Car Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid Electric Car</li><li>Parallel Hybrid Electric Car</li><li>Series-parallel Hybrid Electric Car</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid Electric Cars come in three main market types: Series Hybrid Electric Cars, Parallel Hybrid Electric Cars, and Series-parallel Hybrid Electric Cars. Series hybrids use an electric motor to power the vehicle while a gasoline engine generates electricity to recharge the battery. Parallel hybrids use both the electric motor and gasoline engine to power the vehicle simultaneously. Series-parallel hybrids can switch between using only the electric motor, only the gasoline engine, or both for increased efficiency and performance.</p></p>
