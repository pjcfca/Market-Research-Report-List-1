<p><p>The Eye Allergy Therapeutics market includes different types of treatments for eye allergies. Antihistamines are medications that block the effects of histamine, a chemical released during an allergic reaction. Corticosteroids are anti-inflammatory drugs that reduce inflammation and allergic reactions in the eyes. Immunomodulators are drugs that modify the immune response, helping to prevent and control allergic reactions. These different treatments aim to alleviate symptoms such as itching, redness, and swelling caused by eye allergies.</p></p>
