<p><p>Off-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC) market consists of two main types - Level 2 Charger and Level 3 Charger. Level 2 Chargers are suitable for residential and commercial use, providing a moderate charging speed ideal for overnight charging. Level 3 Chargers, also known as DC fast chargers, are designed for quick charging at public locations, such as highways or parking lots, providing a much faster charging speed compared to Level 2 Chargers. Both types cater to different charging needs and infrastructure requirements in the electric vehicle market.</p></p>
