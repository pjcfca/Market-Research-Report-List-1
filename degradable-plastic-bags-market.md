<p><p>Degradable plastic bags are classified into different market types based on the materials used in their production. These types include Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), Starch Blends, and Others. PLA bags are made from renewable resources such as corn starch and are compostable. PHA bags are produced from natural materials like plant oils and are biodegradable. Starch blends are a combination of starch and other materials, making them both compostable and biodegradable. Other types of degradable plastic bags may include various innovative materials with similar environmentally friendly properties.</p></p>
