<p><strong>The Electrophoretic Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anodic Electrophoretic Coating</li><li>Cathodic Electrophoretic Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrophoretic coating is a method of applying paint to a surface by using an electrical current to attract and deposit the paint particles. There are two main types of electrophoretic coating: Anodic and Cathodic. Anodic electrophoretic coating involves positively charged paint particles being deposited onto a negatively charged surface. In contrast, Cathodic electrophoretic coating involves negatively charged paint particles being attracted to a positively charged surface. Both types offer excellent corrosion resistance and uniform coverage, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
