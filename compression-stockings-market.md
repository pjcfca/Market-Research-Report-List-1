<p><strong>The Compression Stockings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gradient Socks</li><li>Anti-Embolism Socks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compression stockings Market can be broadly divided into two types: Gradient socks and Anti-Embolism socks. Gradient socks are designed to gradually decrease pressure from the ankle up the leg, promoting better blood circulation. These are commonly used for managing symptoms of conditions like varicose veins and edema. On the other hand, Anti-Embolism socks are specifically designed to prevent blood clots during bed rest or post-surgery recovery. Both types of compression stockings serve specific medical purposes and are available in various sizes and styles to suit individual needs.</p></p>
