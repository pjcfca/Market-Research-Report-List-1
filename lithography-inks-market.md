<p><p>Lithography inks are classified into three main types based on their composition: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and others. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and have low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based inks are known for their fast drying time and durability, but they release high levels of VOCs into the environment. Other types of inks include UV-curable, oil-based, and hybrid inks, each offering unique properties and benefits for different printing applications.</p></p>
