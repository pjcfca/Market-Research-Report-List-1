<p><p>Flex LED strip lights come in two main types: 5050 and 3528. The 5050 type has three diodes in one housing, providing brighter and more vibrant lighting. This makes it ideal for applications where high brightness is desired, such as outdoor lighting or accent lighting. On the other hand, the 3528 type has one diode per housing, offering a more subtle and uniform lighting effect. This type is commonly used for indoor lighting where a softer ambiance is preferred. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
