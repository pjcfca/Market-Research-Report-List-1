<p><strong>The Polyethylene Rubber Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene rubber sheets are classified into Type I and Type II markets based on their manufacturing process and properties. Type I sheets are produced using a high-pressure reactor, resulting in high molecular weight and excellent tensile strength. These sheets are ideal for applications requiring durability and abrasion resistance. On the other hand, Type II sheets are made using a low-pressure reactor, providing flexibility and good impact resistance. They are suitable for applications that require elasticity and resilience. Both types cater to different market needs and offer unique advantages.</p></p>
