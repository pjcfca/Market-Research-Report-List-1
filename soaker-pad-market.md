<p><p>Soaker pads are commonly used in the food industry to absorb excess liquids and prevent spoilage of products during transportation and storage. There are three main types of market for soaker pads: Polyethylene (PE), Superabsorbent Polymer (SAP), and Nonwoven Fabric. PE soaker pads are made of polyethylene material and are known for their high absorbency and durability. SAP soaker pads contain superabsorbent polymers that can hold a significant amount of liquid relative to their size. Nonwoven fabric soaker pads are made of fibrous materials bonded together and are valued for their softness and ability to quickly absorb liquids.</p></p>
