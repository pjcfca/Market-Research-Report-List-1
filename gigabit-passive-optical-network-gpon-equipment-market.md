<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment market consists of three main types of components: Optical Line Terminal (OLT), Optical Network Terminal (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. The OLT serves as the central hub that connects the service provider's network to the customer premises. ONTs are installed at the customer's location to connect to the OLT, providing interface for data, voice, and video services. Passive Optical Splitters divide the optical signal from the OLT to multiple ONTs, enabling efficient distribution of services within the network.</p></p>
