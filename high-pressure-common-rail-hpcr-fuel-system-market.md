<p><p>High Pressure Common Rail (HPCR) Fuel System market includes various types such as high-pressure pump, high-pressure lines, rail system, injectors, and Electronic Control Unit (ECU). The high-pressure pump is responsible for pressurizing the fuel, while the high-pressure lines deliver this fuel to the injectors through the rail system. The injectors then spray the fuel into the combustion chamber for efficient combustion. The ECU controls the entire system, ensuring optimal performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
