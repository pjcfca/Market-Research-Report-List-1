<p><p>Digital Video Recorders (DVRs) are available in three main types: Embedded DVRs, Hybrid DVRs, and PC-based DVRs. Embedded DVRs are standalone devices that have a built-in operating system, storage, and software for recording and playback. Hybrid DVRs combine the functionalities of analog and IP cameras, offering flexibility in camera compatibility. PC-based DVRs utilize a computer as the processing unit, providing greater customization and scalability options. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market for digital video recording solutions.</p></p>
