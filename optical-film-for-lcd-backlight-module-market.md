<p><p>Optical films for LCD backlight modules include various types such as reflective film, diffusion film, and lightening film. Reflective film is designed to enhance brightness and contrast by reflecting light back towards the screen. Diffusion film helps to evenly distribute light across the screen, reducing hot spots and providing a more uniform illumination. Lightening film is used to increase overall brightness and clarity, making the display more vibrant and easier to see. Each type of optical film plays a crucial role in optimizing the performance of LCD backlight modules.</p></p>
