<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane fibers are commonly produced through two main spinning processes, Solution Dry Spinning and Solution Wet Spinning. Solution Dry Spinning involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent, which is then evaporated to form the fiber. In Solution Wet Spinning, the polymer is dissolved in a solvent and extruded into a coagulating bath to form the fiber. Other types of polyurethane fibers exist, including melt spinning and gel spinning. Each type of spinning process results in fibers with different properties and applications.</p></p>
