<p><strong>The EPDM Sealing Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfuration</li><li>Thermoplasticity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPDM sealing products are available in two main market types: Sulfuration and Thermoplasticity. Sulfuration involves the process of cross-linking the EPDM material with the use of sulfur, resulting in a durable and heat-resistant sealing product. On the other hand, Thermoplasticity refers to EPDM materials that can be repeatedly melted and solidified, making them flexible and easy to work with. Both types of EPDM sealing products offer unique advantages and cater to different applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
