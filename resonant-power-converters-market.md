<p><p>Resonant power converters have various market types, including DC to high frequency AC inverters and resonant DC-DC converters. DC to high frequency AC inverters convert direct current (DC) into high-frequency alternating current (AC). These inverters are commonly used in applications such as renewable energy systems, industrial power supplies, and electric vehicle powertrains. On the other hand, resonant DC-DC converters efficiently convert DC voltage from one level to another through the use of resonant circuits. These converters find applications in telecommunication equipment, medical devices, and computing systems.</p></p>
