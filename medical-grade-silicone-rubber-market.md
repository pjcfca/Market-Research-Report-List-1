<p><strong>The Medical-Grade Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Consistency Rubber (HCR)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical-grade silicone rubber is divided into two main types: High Consistency Rubber (HCR) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HCR is a solid material that is processed using traditional methods like compression or transfer molding, making it suitable for applications requiring high tear strength and abrasion resistance. LSR, on the other hand, is a liquid material that is injection molded, allowing for intricate designs and fast production cycles. LSR is often used in applications where a high level of precision and biocompatibility is required.</p></p>
