<p><p>Universal I/O modules are versatile devices used in various industries for collecting and transmitting data. Digital I/O modules deal with binary inputs and outputs, processing signals in an on/off manner. Analog I/O modules, on the other hand, handle continuous signals and can process a range of values. Digital modules are commonly used for simple control applications, while analog modules are preferred for more complex tasks requiring precise measurements or variable control. Both types play a crucial role in industrial automation and control systems.</p></p>
