<p><strong>The TPV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM/PP Blends</li><li>NR/PP Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TPV market types such as EPDM/PP blends and NR/PP blends offer rubber-like characteristics combined with the processing ease of plastics. EPDM/PP blends combine the properties of EPDM rubber and polypropylene, offering good weather resistance and high-temperature performance. On the other hand, NR/PP blends combine natural rubber with polypropylene to provide good flexibility and impact resistance. Both types are commonly used in automotive, construction, and industrial applications where a balance of rubber-like properties and processability is required.</p></p>
