<p><p>The Veterinary Fluid Management Equipment Market comprises two main types: Large-Volume Infusion Pumps and Syringe Pumps. Large-Volume Infusion Pumps are used to administer fluids in large amounts to animals, allowing for precise control and consistent delivery of medications or solutions. On the other hand, Syringe Pumps are designed for smaller volume infusions and are often used for accurate and controlled delivery of drugs or fluids at low rates, suitable for animals with specific medical needs. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient and safe management of fluids in veterinary practice.</p></p>
