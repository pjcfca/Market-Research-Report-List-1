<p><p>Semiconductor process tapes are used in the manufacturing of semiconductor devices to protect delicate components during the fabrication process. There are two main types of these tapes: UV Tape and Non-UV Tape. UV Tape offers high transparency and excellent adhesion properties, making it suitable for applications that require precise alignment. On the other hand, Non-UV Tape does not require UV exposure for curing and is more cost-effective. Both types of tapes play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity of semiconductor devices by preventing contamination and damage during production.</p></p>
