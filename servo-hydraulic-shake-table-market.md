<p><p>Servo hydraulic shake tables are advanced testing equipment used for simulating various real-life vibrations and motions. There are two main types of servo hydraulic shake tables: multi-axis shake table and single-axis shake table. Multi-axis shake tables can simulate vibrations in multiple directions simultaneously, providing more comprehensive testing capabilities. On the other hand, single-axis shake tables can only simulate vibrations in one direction at a time. Both types of shake tables are essential in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and earthquake engineering for testing the structural integrity of various components and systems under different vibration scenarios.</p></p>
