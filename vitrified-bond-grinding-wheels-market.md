<p><p>Vitrified bond grinding wheels are classified into two main types: Ceramic Grinding Wheel and Ceramic Super Hard Grinding Wheel. Ceramic grinding wheels are made of ceramic grains bonded together with a vitrified bond, offering excellent performance in grinding operations. On the other hand, ceramic super hard grinding wheels are designed with super hard abrasives like cubic boron nitride (CBN) or diamond particles, providing superior cutting ability and longer lifespan. Both types of wheels are widely used in precision grinding applications across various industries.</p></p>
