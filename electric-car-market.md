<p><p>The electric car market consists of two main types of vehicles: plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs) and battery electric vehicles (BEVs). PHEVs have both an electric motor and a conventional gasoline engine, allowing them to switch between the two power sources. BEVs, on the other hand, run solely on electricity stored in their batteries. PHEVs offer more flexibility with longer driving ranges, while BEVs are more environmentally friendly with zero emissions. Both types are gaining popularity as technology and infrastructure for electric vehicles continue to improve.</p></p>
