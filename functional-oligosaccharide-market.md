<p><strong>The Functional Oligosaccharide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Galacto Oligosaccharides</li><li>Fructo Oligosaccharides</li><li>Mannan Oligosaccharides</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional oligosaccharides are categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Galacto-oligosaccharides (GOS) are derived from lactose and are known for their prebiotic effects. Fructo-oligosaccharides (FOS) are naturally found in plants and act as soluble fibers. Mannan oligosaccharides (MOS) are derived from yeast cell walls and are used as immune enhancers. Other types of functional oligosaccharides include isomaltose, xylo-oligosaccharides, and soybean oligosaccharides, each with their own unique health benefits.</p></p>
