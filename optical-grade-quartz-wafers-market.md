<p><p>Optical Grade Quartz Wafers are available in two main types: Single Side Polished and Double Side Polished. Single Side Polished wafers have one side polished to a high degree of flatness and smoothness, making them suitable for applications where only one side requires optical quality. On the other hand, Double Side Polished wafers have both sides polished to ensure high optical quality on both surfaces, making them ideal for applications where transparency and clarity are crucial. Both types cater to different requirements in the optical industry.</p></p>
