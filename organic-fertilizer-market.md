<p><p>Organic fertilizer market types can be categorized as plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based fertilizers. Plant-based fertilizers are derived from plant material and are rich in nutrients like nitrogen and potassium. Animal-based fertilizers are made from animal waste or byproducts and provide nutrients like phosphorus and calcium. Mineral-based fertilizers are derived from naturally occurring minerals and contain essential nutrients like sulfur and magnesium. Each type of organic fertilizer offers different benefits for plant growth and soil health.</p></p>
