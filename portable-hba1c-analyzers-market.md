<p><ul><li>Ion-exchange HPLC Testing Method</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate Affinity Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable HbA1c analyzers utilize different testing methods to measure hemoglobin A1c levels. Ion-exchange HPLC testing method is a high-performance liquid chromatography technique that separates and quantifies different hemoglobin types. Immunoassay method uses antibodies to detect and measure hemoglobin A1c. Boronate affinity technology utilizes boron-based compounds to specifically bind to hemoglobin A1c for measurement. Each method offers unique advantages and applications for portable HbA1c analyzers, providing healthcare professionals with options to accurately monitor and manage diabetes.</p></p>
