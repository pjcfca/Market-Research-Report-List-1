<p><ul><li>Proportional Valve Driver</li><li>Solenoid Valve Driver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valve driver market consists of two main types: Proportional Valve Driver and Solenoid Valve Driver. Proportional Valve Driver controls the flow rate of fluid or gas through a valve by varying the input signal, offering precise control and accuracy. On the other hand, Solenoid Valve Driver operates on/off valves using an electromechanical solenoid to open or close the valve. Both types cater to different applications based on the specific requirements for control and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
