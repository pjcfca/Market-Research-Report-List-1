<p><p>Signaling System 7 (SS7) is a communication protocol used in telecommunications networks. It comprises several market types, including:</p><p>1. Access Link: Also known as A Link, it connects a signaling point to a signaling transfer point.</p><p>2. Bridge Link: Connects two signaling points directly without going through any signaling transfer point.</p><p>3. Cross Link: Connects two signaling transfer points in different signaling points.</p><p>4. Diagonal Link: Connects signaling points and signaling transfer points in a diagonal manner.</p><p>5. Extended Link: Extends the range of a link beyond its normal operational limits.</p><p>6. Fully Associated Link: Establishes a direct, bidirectional link between signaling points.</p></p>
