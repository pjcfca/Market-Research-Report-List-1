<p><p>Thermal conductive sheets are used to efficiently transfer heat between electronic components and heatsinks. There are three main types of thermal conductive sheets: Carbon Fiber Type, Silicone Type, and Acrylic Type. Carbon Fiber Type sheets offer high thermal conductivity and mechanical strength. Silicone Type sheets provide flexibility and are easy to apply. Acrylic Type sheets are known for their good insulation properties. Each type offers unique advantages, allowing manufacturers to choose the best option based on their specific requirements for thermal management in electronic devices.</p></p>
