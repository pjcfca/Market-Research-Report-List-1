<p><p>The truck fastener market can be broadly categorized into two types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners include bolts, screws, nuts, and studs, which are used to secure two or more components together using threads. Non-threaded fasteners, on the other hand, include rivets, pins, clips, and washers, which do not require threads for installation. Both types of fasteners play a crucial role in ensuring the structural integrity and safety of trucks and other heavy vehicles.</p></p>
