<p><p>Bio-plasticizers market types include Epoxidized Soybean Oil (ESBO), Castor Oil, Citrates, Succinic Acid, and others. ESBO is derived from soybean oil and is commonly used in PVC products. Castor oil is a renewable and non-toxic plasticizer alternative. Citrates are esters of citric acid and are biodegradable. Succinic acid is a bio-based plasticizer that can replace traditional phthalates. Other bio-plasticizers include adipates, glycerol esters, and polycarbonates, which are derived from renewable sources and offer eco-friendly alternatives in the plastics industry.</p></p>
