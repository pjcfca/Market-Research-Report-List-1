<p><p>Resealable labels are used in various types of packaging markets such as Flow Wrap Pack, Tray Seal, Gas-flush Thermoforming, HPP Packs, and Vacuum Packs. Flow Wrap Pack involves sealing the product in a flexible film. Tray Seal utilizes a plastic tray with a film lid. Gas-flush Thermoforming involves removing air from the package and replacing it with a protective gas. HPP Packs are high-pressure processed for food preservation. Vacuum Packs remove air from the package to extend shelf life.</p></p>
