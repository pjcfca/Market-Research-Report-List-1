<p><ul><li>LDPE Heat Shrink Film</li><li>LLDPE Heat Shrink Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) Heat Shrink Films are used for packaging goods as they shrink tightly over the products when heat is applied. Two main types of PE Heat Shrink Films are LDPE Heat Shrink Film and LLDPE Heat Shrink Film. LDPE Heat Shrink Films are known for their flexibility and clarity, making them ideal for wrapping irregularly shaped items. LLDPE Heat Shrink Films, on the other hand, offer high tensile strength and puncture resistance, making them suitable for heavier or sharper-edged products. Both types provide effective protection and secure packaging for various items.</p></p>
