<p><strong>The Tungsten Ore Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wolframite</li><li>Scheelite</li><li>Ferberite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tungsten ore is primarily found in three main market types: wolframite, scheelite, and ferberite. Wolframite is a solid solution series between the minerals ferberite and hubnerite, commonly found in high-temperature hydrothermal veins. Scheelite is a calcium tungstate mineral often occurring in skarn deposits. Ferberite is an iron tungstate mineral that commonly forms in granite pegmatites. Each market type has distinct characteristics and occurrences, making them unique sources of tungsten ore for industrial applications.</p></p>
