<p><p>Electrode foils are essential components in various electronic devices. The cathode foil is typically used in batteries and capacitors, serving as the positive electrode. It provides a stable platform for the deposition of active materials and helps facilitate efficient electron transfer during the battery's operation. On the other hand, the anode foil functions as the negative electrode in these devices. It plays a crucial role in storing and releasing energy during the charge and discharge cycles, contributing to the overall performance of the battery or capacitor.</p></p>
