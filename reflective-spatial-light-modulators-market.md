<p><strong>The Reflective Spatial Light Modulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dielectric Mirror Type</li><li>No-Dielectric Mirror Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators are used in various applications such as beam steering, holography, and optical trapping. Dielectric Mirror Type modulators use a multilayer dielectric stack to reflect light, providing high optical efficiency and durability. On the other hand, No-Dielectric Mirror Type modulators use a combination of thin metal films and liquid crystals to modulate light. These modulators offer improved contrast ratio and wider spectral range compared to dielectric mirror types. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for spatial light modulation technology.</p></p>
