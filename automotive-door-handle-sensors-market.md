<p><p>The Automotive Door Handle Sensors market consists of two main types - Button Type and Induction Type. Button Type sensors require physical contact with a button to operate the door handle, providing a tactile response to the user. On the other hand, Induction Type sensors use proximity technology to detect the user's presence and automatically unlock the door handle without any physical contact. Both types offer convenience and improved safety in vehicle access, catering to different consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
