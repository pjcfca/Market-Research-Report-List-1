<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Airport IT Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AOCC</li><li>DCS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport IT systems are categorized into different types, such as AOCC (Airport Operations Control Center) and DCS (Departure Control Systems). AOCC is a central hub that integrates and manages various airport operations, including security, communications, and flight planning. It enables coordination and decision-making for efficient airport functioning. On the other hand, DCS focuses on passenger-related tasks, such as check-in, boarding, and baggage handling. It ensures smooth departure processes, allowing airlines to efficiently manage flights and passenger services. Both systems are integral for maintaining airport operations and enhancing passenger experience.</p></p>
