<p><p>The automotive wet clutch market consists of two main types: single wet clutch and multiple wet clutch. A single wet clutch refers to a clutch system that has only one clutch disc, which engages and disengages power delivery from the engine to the transmission. On the other hand, a multiple wet clutch consists of two or more clutch discs, allowing for more precise control over power transfer. Both types are commonly used in various vehicles to provide smooth and efficient shifting of gears.</p></p>
