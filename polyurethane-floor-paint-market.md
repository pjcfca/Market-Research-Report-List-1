<p><strong>The Polyurethane Floor Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint</li><li>Non-Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint is a type of paint that contains volatile organic compounds (VOCs) which evaporate into the air as the paint dries. This type of paint requires proper ventilation during application due to its strong odor and harmful emissions. On the other hand, Non-Solvent Polyurethane Floor Paint is a more environmentally friendly option as it does not contain VOCs, making it safer for both the environment and human health. This type of paint offers a low odor and quick drying time, making it a popular choice for indoor applications.</p></p>
