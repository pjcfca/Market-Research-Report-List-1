<p><p>Dispensing valves are used in various industries for accurate and controlled dispensing of fluids and adhesives. Spray valves are commonly used for dispensing fine mist or spray patterns. Needle dispensing valves are used for precise dispensing of fluids in small amounts. Diaphragm glue valves are ideal for dispensing high viscosity materials like adhesives and sealants. Screw dispensing valves are designed for dispensing thicker fluids like solder paste or grease. Each type of dispensing valve caters to specific dispensing requirements in different industries.</p></p>
