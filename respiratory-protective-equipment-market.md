<p><p>Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) market consists of two main types: Air-Purifying Respirators (APR) and Supplied Air Respirators (SAR). APRs use filters or cartridges to remove contaminants from the air before it is inhaled, while SARs supply clean air from an uncontaminated source. APRs are suitable for environments with low levels of contaminants, while SARs are recommended for more hazardous conditions where a portable air source is needed. Both types play a crucial role in protecting workers from respiratory hazards in various industries.</p></p>
