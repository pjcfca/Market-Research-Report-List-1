<p><p>Printing inks for flexible packaging can be categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and UV-curable. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly and offer good color vibrancy. Solvent-based inks are known for their durability and resistance to scratching and rubbing. UV-curable inks are fast-drying and provide high gloss finishes. Each type of ink has its own unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different printing requirements and preferences in the flexible packaging industry.</p></p>
