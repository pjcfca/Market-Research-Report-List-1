<p><p>Foundry chemicals market includes various types such as binders, additive agents, coatings, fluxes, hot topping compounds, and others. Binders are used to bond the sand particles together while additive agents are added to improve the properties of the mold or core. Coatings are applied to the surface of the mold to improve the surface finish of the final casting. Fluxes are used to remove impurities from the molten metal. Hot topping compounds are used to prevent oxidation of the metal during casting. Other chemicals may include degassing agents, curing agents, and mold release agents.</p></p>
