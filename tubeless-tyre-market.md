<p><p>Tubeless tyres are divided into two main categories: Radial Tubeless Tyres and Bias Tubeless Tyres. Radial tyres have steel belts running at right angles to the direction of travel, providing better stability and fuel efficiency. Bias tyres have fabric belts running at 30-45 degree angles to the direction of travel, known for their strong sidewalls and ability to carry heavy loads. Both types offer advantages in terms of performance, durability, and safety, catering to different vehicle requirements and preferences.</p></p>
