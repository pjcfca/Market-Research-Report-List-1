<p><strong>The UV LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV LED market can be divided into three main types based on the wavelength of ultraviolet light emitted: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs typically emit light in the range of 320-400nm and are commonly used for applications like curing, counterfeit detection, and tanning. UV-B LEDs emit light in the range of 280-320nm and find applications in medical treatment, phototherapy, and plant growth. UV-C LEDs emit light in the range of 100-280nm and are used for sterilization, water purification, and air disinfection.</p></p>
