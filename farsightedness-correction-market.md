<p><p>Farsightedness correction market offers different types of solutions, including corneal inlays and scleral implants. Corneal inlays are small devices inserted into the cornea to improve near vision, allowing light to properly focus on the retina. Scleral implants, on the other hand, involve placing small plastic discs in the inner white part of the eye to correct farsightedness. Both methods aim to provide better vision for individuals suffering from farsightedness by modifying the way light is refracted and reaching the retina.</p></p>
