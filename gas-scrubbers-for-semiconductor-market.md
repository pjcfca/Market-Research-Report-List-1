<p><p>Gas scrubbers for the semiconductor market are used to remove harmful gases and particles from exhaust gases during semiconductor manufacturing processes. Burn scrubbers utilize high temperatures to thermally destroy these harmful gases. Plasma scrubbers use plasma to convert the gases into less harmful substances. Heat wet scrubbers use water or other liquids to absorb and neutralize the gases. Dry scrubbers use chemicals or absorbents to capture and remove the gases. These different types of scrubbers offer various methods of purification to ensure the safe release of exhaust gases in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
