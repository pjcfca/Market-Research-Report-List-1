<p><p>Body armor vests are typically classified into two main categories: soft armor and hard armor. Soft armor vests are made from flexible materials such as Kevlar or other fabrics, providing protection against handgun rounds and other low-velocity threats. On the other hand, hard armor vests are constructed with rigid materials like ceramic or metal plates, offering superior protection against higher-velocity ammunition and rifle rounds. Both types of armor vests serve the crucial purpose of safeguarding individuals in law enforcement, military, and security professions.</p></p>
