<p><p>The ETFE Tubing Market can be broadly categorized into two types: Heat Shrinkable Tubing and Extruded Tubing. Heat Shrinkable Tubing is designed to shrink in diameter when heat is applied, providing a tight and secure fit around the object it is covering. Extruded Tubing, on the other hand, is manufactured through a process where the material is forced through a die to create the desired shape and size of tubing. Both types of tubing offer unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
