<p><p>Wire and cable polymer market includes various types of materials such as thermoplastic resin, thermoplastic elastomer, thermoset elastomer, and polymer fiber. Thermoplastic resin is a type of polymer that softens when heated and hardens when cooled, making it ideal for molding processes. Thermoplastic elastomer combines the properties of rubber with the processability of plastic. Thermoset elastomer remains in a permanently solid state after being cured, offering high resistance to heat and chemicals. Polymer fiber is a type of synthetic material used for strength and durability in wire and cable applications.</p></p>
