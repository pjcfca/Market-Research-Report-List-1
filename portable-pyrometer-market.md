<p><p>Portable pyrometers are widely used in various industries for measuring high temperatures without contact. There are two main types of portable pyrometers available in the market - Optical Technology and Infrared Technology. Optical pyrometers measure temperature by comparing the brightness of a heated object to that of a calibrated light source. Infrared pyrometers detect temperature by measuring the amount of infrared energy emitted by an object. Both types offer accurate temperature measurements and are suitable for different applications depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
