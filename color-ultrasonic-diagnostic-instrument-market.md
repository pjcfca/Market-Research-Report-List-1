<p><p>A Color Ultrasonic Diagnostic Instrument is a medical device that uses ultrasonic waves to create images of internal body structures. It is widely used in various medical applications, including cardiology, radiology, obstetrics, and gynecology. The instrument works by sending high-frequency sound waves into the body and receiving the echoes that bounce back, which are then converted into visual images.</p><p>The Color Ultrasonic Diagnostic Instrument Market has seen significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Color ultrasonic diagnostic instruments offer non-invasive and real-time imaging capabilities, making them valuable tools in the diagnosis and treatment of such diseases.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing geriatric population across the globe is also contributing to the market growth. <p><p>The Color Ultrasonic Diagnostic Instrument Market consists of two types: Portable and Non-portable.
