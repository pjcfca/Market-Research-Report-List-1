<p><p>Marine Life Jacket Market Types include Buoyant Material Filled Life Jackets and Inflatable Life Jackets. Buoyant material filled life jackets are made with materials like foam or Kapok that provide buoyancy when submerged in water. On the other hand, Inflatable life jackets use air chambers that can be inflated manually or automatically to provide buoyancy. Both types are designed to keep individuals afloat in water and are essential safety equipment for water activities like boating, fishing, and water sports.</p></p>
