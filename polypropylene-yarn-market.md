<p><ul><li>PP Multifilament</li><li>PP Monofilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene yarn market consists of two main types: PP Multifilament and PP Monofilament. PP Multifilament yarns are made up of multiple fine threads twisted together, providing strength, durability, and a smooth surface finish. PP Monofilament yarns, on the other hand, are made up of a single thick thread, offering high tensile strength and abrasion resistance. Both types are widely used in industries such as textiles, automotive, and agriculture for applications requiring flexibility, elasticity, and toughness.</p></p>
