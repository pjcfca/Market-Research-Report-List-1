<p><p>Steel cord for radial tires can be classified into different types based on their tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords are commonly used in standard tires, while High Tensile (HT) cords offer increased strength for heavy-duty applications. Super Tensile (ST) cords have even higher strength capabilities, ideal for demanding conditions. Ultra Tensile (UT) cords provide the greatest tensile strength, suitable for extreme conditions such as off-road or military vehicles. Each type of steel cord caters to specific tire requirements to ensure optimal performance and durability.</p></p>
