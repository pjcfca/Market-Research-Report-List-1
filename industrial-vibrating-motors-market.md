<p><ul><li>AC Vibrating Motors</li><li>DC Vibrating Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC vibrating motors are powered by alternating current and are commonly used in applications where continuous and reliable vibration is required, such as in feeders, screens, and conveyors. DC vibrating motors, on the other hand, are powered by direct current and are suitable for applications where variable speed control is needed, such as in portable equipment and handheld devices. Both types of motors play a crucial role in industrial processes by providing efficient and controlled vibration for various machinery and equipment.</p></p>
