<p><p>The car DVR market is divided into single channel and dual channel types. Single channel DVRs are equipped with one camera that records the front view of the vehicle, while dual channel DVRs come with two cameras to record both the front and rear views simultaneously. Single channel DVRs are popular among those who primarily want to record the road ahead, while dual channel DVRs are preferred by drivers who want a more comprehensive recording of their surroundings for security purposes.</p></p>
