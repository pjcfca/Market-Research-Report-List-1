<p><p>Insecticides waste management involves various methods such as Mechanical Biological Treatment (MBT), Incineration, and Anaerobic Digestion. MBT combines mechanical sorting with biological treatment to separate and break down waste. Incineration involves burning waste at high temperatures to reduce volume and destroy harmful substances. Anaerobic Digestion uses microorganisms to break down organic matter in the absence of oxygen, producing biogas as a byproduct. Each method offers a different approach to managing insecticides waste effectively and sustainably.</p></p>
