<p><strong>The Legal Protection Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Before the Event (BTE) Insurance</li><li>After the Event (ATE) Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Before the Event (BTE) Insurance is a type of legal protection insurance that provides coverage before any potential legal dispute arises. It typically covers legal costs and expenses for various legal matters, such as employment disputes or personal injury claims. After the Event (ATE) Insurance, on the other hand, is a type of legal protection insurance that is taken out after a legal dispute arises. It covers the costs and expenses of the legal action, such as court fees and opponent's legal costs, if the insured party is unsuccessful in the case.</p></p>
