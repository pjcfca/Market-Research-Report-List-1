<p><p>Saline nasal sprays, which are made from a saltwater solution, come in two main types: isotonic sea water nasal spray and hypertonic sea water nasal spray. Isotonic sea water nasal spray has a salt concentration similar to that of the body's cells, making it gentle and ideal for everyday nasal irrigation and moisturizing. Hypertonic sea water nasal spray, on the other hand, has a higher salt concentration, making it more effective for clearing congestion and reducing inflammation in the nasal passages. Both types can help soothe nasal discomfort and promote better breathing.</p></p>
