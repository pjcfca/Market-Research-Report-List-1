<p><p>Abrasive Polishing Fluid market comprises two main types: Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid and Chemical Action Polishing Fluid. Mechanical Action Polishing Fluid involves the use of abrasive particles to physically remove imperfections from the surface being polished. On the other hand, Chemical Action Polishing Fluid utilizes chemical compounds to dissolve or chemically alter the surface, resulting in a smoother finish. Both types of polishing fluids play a crucial role in achieving desired surface finishes in various industries.</p></p>
