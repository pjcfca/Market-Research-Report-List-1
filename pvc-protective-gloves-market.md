<p><p>PVC protective gloves are available in two main types: powdered PVC gloves and powder-free PVC gloves. Powdered PVC gloves are coated with cornstarch to make them easier to put on and take off. They are commonly used in industries where workers need a firm grip and protection against chemicals. On the other hand, powder-free PVC gloves do not contain any cornstarch and are preferred in industries where powder contamination needs to be avoided. Both types offer protection against chemicals, abrasions, and punctures.</p></p>
