<p><strong>The Automotive Active Window Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combiner Technology</li><li>Windshield Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Window Display market is segmented into two main types: Combiner Technology and Windshield Technology. </p><p>Combiner Technology involves projecting information onto a separate display panel located in the driver's line of sight, typically mounted on the dashboard. This technology allows for easy viewing of critical information while driving.</p><p>Windshield Technology, on the other hand, projects information directly onto the windshield itself, creating a heads-up display for the driver. This technology enhances safety by keeping the driver's eyes on the road.</p></p>
