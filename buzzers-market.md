<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buzzers are available in two main market types: Electromagnetic and Piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzers use an electromagnet to produce sound when activated by an electric current, making them suitable for applications where a loud and clear tone is required. In contrast, Piezoelectric buzzers generate sound through the vibration of a ceramic disk when an electric signal is applied, making them ideal for applications where a compact size and low power consumption are important. Both types offer unique advantages based on their operating principles.</p></p>
