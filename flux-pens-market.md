<p><strong>The Flux Pens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rosin Activated (RA) Flux Pens</li><li>No Clean (NC) Flux Pens</li><li>Water Soluble (WS) Flux Pens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flux Pens are available in three main types: Rosin Activated (RA), No Clean (NC), and Water Soluble (WS). RA Flux Pens contain rosin that is activated by heat to remove oxides from metal surfaces during soldering. NC Flux Pens leave behind minimal residue after soldering, eliminating the need for cleaning. WS Flux Pens leave a water-soluble residue that can be easily removed with water. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the electronics manufacturing industry.</p></p>
