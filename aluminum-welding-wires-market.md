<p><p>Aluminum welding wires market can be classified into three main types: Pure Aluminum Welding Wire, Aluminum-Magnesium Alloy Welding Wire, and Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire. Pure Aluminum Welding Wire is made of high purity aluminum and is suitable for welding aluminum sheets and plates. Aluminum-Magnesium Alloy Welding Wire is a popular choice for welding aluminum-magnesium alloys due to its high strength and resistance to corrosion. Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire is used for welding aluminum-silicon alloys, offering good fluidity and crack resistance during the welding process.</p></p>
