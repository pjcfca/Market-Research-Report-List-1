<p><strong>The Electronic Design Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>System Level</li><li>Circuit Level</li><li>Physical Implementation Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic design software market can be categorized into three major types: system level, circuit level, and physical implementation level. System level software allows engineers to design and simulate the behavior and functionality of an entire electronic system. Circuit level software enables engineers to design and simulate electronic circuits, including individual components and connections. Physical implementation level software helps engineers to design and optimize the physical layout of the electronic components on a printed circuit board (PCB) or integrated circuit (IC), including routing and placement.</p></p>
