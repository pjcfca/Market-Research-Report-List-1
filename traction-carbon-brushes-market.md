<p><ul><li>Metal Graphite</li><li>Natural Graphite</li><li>Electrochemical Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traction carbon brushes are used in various industries for power transmission applications. There are three types of traction carbon brushes available: metal graphite, natural graphite, and electrochemical graphite. Metal graphite brushes are made by impregnating graphite with metal, making them sturdy and suitable for heavy-duty applications. Natural graphite brushes are primarily made of natural graphite material and are typically used for lighter load operations. Electrochemical graphite brushes are designed for high-speed applications and are made by enhancing the electrical conductivity of the graphite material through a special electrochemical process.</p></p>
