<p><p>The E-Commerce Payment Market encompasses several types of payment methods. Real-time bank transfers allow instant payment from the customer's online bank account. Offline bank transfers involve customers making a payment at a physical bank or via ATM. Cash on delivery allows customers to pay in cash when their order is delivered. Direct debits authorize merchants to deduct funds directly from the customer's bank account. E-invoices are digital invoices sent to customers for payment. E-wallets store a customer's payment information for easy online transactions. PostPay allows customers to pay after receiving the product. PrePay involves customers preloading funds into an account for future purchases. Pre-paid cards are pre-loaded with a specific amount for online payments. Other methods include gift cards, mobile payments, and cryptocurrencies.</p></p>
