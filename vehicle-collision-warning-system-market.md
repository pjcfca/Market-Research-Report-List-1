<p><p>The Vehicle Collision Warning System market includes four main types: Forward Collision Warning System, Lane Departure Warning System, Pedestrian Detection System, and Automatic Braking System. The Forward Collision Warning System alerts drivers of potential front-end collisions, while the Lane Departure Warning System warns of unintended lane departures. The Pedestrian Detection System identifies pedestrians in the vehicle's path, and the Automatic Braking System automatically applies the brakes to prevent or mitigate collisions. These systems aim to enhance overall road safety and reduce accident rates.</p></p>
