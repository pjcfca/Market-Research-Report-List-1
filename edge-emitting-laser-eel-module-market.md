<p><p>The Edge Emitting Laser (EEL) module market consists of various types of lasers, including Distributed Feedback Laser, Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser, Fabry-Perot Laser, and Broad Area Laser Diodes. Distributed Feedback Laser offers single-frequency operation, high spectral purity, and narrow linewidth. Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser provides high power and wavelength stability. Fabry-Perot Laser is known for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Broad Area Laser Diodes offer high output power and wide beam divergence. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the laser industry.</p></p>
