<p><ul><li>Plastic Sheath</li><li>Al Sheath</li><li>Composite Sheath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway digital signal cables are available in three main types based on their sheathing material: Plastic sheath, Al sheath, and Composite sheath. Plastic sheath cables are cost-effective and offer basic protection. Al sheath cables provide higher durability and electromagnetic interference protection. Composite sheath cables combine the benefits of both plastic and Al sheath cables, offering a balance between cost-efficiency and performance. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different railway signal applications based on the level of protection and environmental factors.</p></p>
