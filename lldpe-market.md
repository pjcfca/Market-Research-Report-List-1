<p><strong>The LLDPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C4- LLDPE</li><li>C6- LLDPE</li><li>C8- LLDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LLDPE market types can be divided into three categories based on the type of comonomer used in their production process: C4-LLDPE, C6-LLDPE, and C8-LLDPE. C4-LLDPE is produced using butene as a comonomer, C6-LLDPE is produced using hexene, and C8-LLDPE is produced using octene. Each type of LLDPE has its own unique properties and applications, with C4-LLDPE generally being more flexible, C6-LLDPE offering improved impact resistance, and C8-LLDPE providing better tear resistance.</p></p>
