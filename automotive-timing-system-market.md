<p><p>The automotive timing system market consists of two main types - timing belts and timing chains. Timing belts are made of rubber and are quieter but less durable than timing chains, which are made of metal. Timing belts are typically lighter and more affordable, while timing chains are heavier and last longer. Both are crucial components of an engine, ensuring that the valves open and close at the right time in sync with the movement of the pistons for optimal engine performance.</p></p>
