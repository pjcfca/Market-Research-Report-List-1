<p><p>LED drivers are crucial components in LED lighting systems to regulate the power supply and ensure proper functioning of the LEDs. There are two main types of LED drivers in the market: internal drivers and external drivers. Internal drivers are integrated directly into the LED fixture, making them more compact and ideal for residential and commercial applications. External drivers, on the other hand, are separate devices that are mounted outside the fixture, allowing for more flexibility and customization in lighting designs for industrial and outdoor settings.</p></p>
