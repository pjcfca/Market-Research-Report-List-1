<p><p>HDMI Optical Transceivers are classified into two main types based on the type of fiber they support: Single Mode Fiber and Double Mode Fiber. Single Mode Fiber transceivers are designed for long-distance transmission with a single light wave, providing high performance over extended distances. On the other hand, Double Mode Fiber transceivers support multiple light waves for shorter distances, offering increased bandwidth. Both types of transceivers cater to different needs in the market depending on the distance and bandwidth requirements of the application.</p></p>
