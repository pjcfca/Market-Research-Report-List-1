<p><p>Agricultural limestone can be categorized into three main types: calcitic, dolomitic, and other markets. Calcitic limestone primarily contains calcium carbonate, which is beneficial for increasing soil pH and improving nutrient availability for crops. Dolomitic limestone contains both calcium carbonate and magnesium carbonate, providing additional magnesium for plants. Other types of agricultural limestone may include specialized blends or products with unique properties for specific soil and crop needs. Each type of limestone offers varying benefits for soil health and crop productivity.</p></p>
