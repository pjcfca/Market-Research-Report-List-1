<p><p>The hydrogen car market consists of two main types: HICEV (Hydrogen Internal Combustion Engine Vehicle) and FCEV (Fuel Cell Vehicle). HICEVs use hydrogen as fuel for an internal combustion engine similar to traditional gasoline cars. On the other hand, FCEVs utilize a fuel cell to convert hydrogen into electricity to power an electric motor. Both types of vehicles offer a sustainable alternative to traditional gasoline-powered cars, with FCEVs being considered more efficient and environmentally friendly in the long run.</p></p>
