<p><p>Melamine laminate is a type of decorative surfacing material that is commonly used in the production of furniture, cabinets, countertops, and other interior components. Melamine laminate is known for its high durability, scratch resistance, and easy maintenance, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.</p><p>The global melamine laminate market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for aesthetically pleasing and durable interior surfaces.
