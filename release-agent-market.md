<p><strong>The Release Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Mold Releases</li><li>External Mold Releases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Release agents are used in mold-making processes to prevent sticking of the molded product to the mold surface. There are two main types of release agents: Internal Mold Releases and External Mold Releases. Internal mold releases are mixed into the molding material itself, providing easy release during demolding. External mold releases are applied directly to the mold surface before each molding cycle. Both types ensure smooth demolding without damaging the mold or the molded product, increasing efficiency and product quality in various industries.</p></p>
