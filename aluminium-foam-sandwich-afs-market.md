<p><p>Aluminium Foam Sandwich (AFS) is a lightweight structural material composed of two face sheets bonded to a foam core. There are two main types of AFS: ex-situ bonded AFS, where the face sheets are bonded to the foam core before assembly, and in-situ bonded AFS, where the face sheets are bonded to the foam core during the assembly process. Both types offer high strength-to-weight ratios and are used in a variety of industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
