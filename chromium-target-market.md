<p><p>Chromium targets are used in various applications, with two main types being plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are fixed in place and ideal for uniform sputtering, while rotating targets spin during the deposition process, allowing for more even coverage and longer target life. Both types cater to different market needs - plane targets are commonly used in flat panel display production, while rotating targets are favored in industries such as solar cell manufacturing for their efficiency and precision.</p></p>
