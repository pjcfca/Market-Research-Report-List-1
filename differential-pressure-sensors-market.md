<p><p>Differential pressure sensors can be classified into two types: digital type and analog type. Digital type sensors provide output in digital format, offering greater accuracy and precision in measuring pressure differences. They are commonly used in advanced applications that require precise control and monitoring. On the other hand, analog type sensors provide output in analog format, which is suitable for applications where real-time monitoring and control are essential. Both types cater to different market needs based on the level of accuracy and precision required.</p></p>
