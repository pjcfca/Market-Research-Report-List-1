<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Belt Drives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ZV</li><li>CV</li><li>CR</li><li>CL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belt drives are commonly classified into four main types - ZV, CV, CR, and CL. ZV belts are narrow belts with a trapezoidal cross-section, suitable for high-speed applications. CV belts have a narrow, rectangular cross-section and are ideal for high-power transmission. CR belts are cogged belts, providing better grip and reduced slip. CL belts are long, classic V-belts used in various industrial machinery applications. Each type of belt drive offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the system.</p></p>
