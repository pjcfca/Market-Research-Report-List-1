<p><p>The fiberglass market can be classified into different types based on the form of glass fibers used. Roving Glass Fibers are continuous strands of fibers suitable for reinforcing composites. Chopped Glass Fibers are short lengths that are commonly used in injection molding and thermoplastic applications. Yarn Glass Fibers are twisted strands used for textile applications. Other types in the market include mats, fabrics, and tapes which are used in various industries for reinforcement purposes. Each type serves unique purposes and applications in the fiberglass industry.</p></p>
