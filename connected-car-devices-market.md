<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Connected Car Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V)</li><li>Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I)</li><li>Vehicle-to-Pedestrian(V2P)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected car devices are categorized into three main types: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V), Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I), and Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) markets. V2V technology enables communication between vehicles on the road to share real-time information such as speed and location. V2I connects vehicles with infrastructure like traffic lights and road signs to improve traffic flow. V2P allows cars to communicate with pedestrians, enhancing safety by providing alerts and warnings to both drivers and pedestrians. These technologies collectively aim to create a more efficient and safer driving environment.</p></p>
