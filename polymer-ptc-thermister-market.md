<p><p>Polymer PTC thermistors are commonly found in two primary market types: surface mount type and radial leaded type. Surface mount type thermistors are compact and provide high electrical performance in a small package, making them ideal for modern electronics and circuit boards. On the other hand, radial leaded type thermistors have longer lead wires that can easily be inserted into through-holes on a PCB, making them more versatile for different applications. Both types offer reliable temperature sensing and overcurrent protection in various electronic devices.</p></p>
